▶  INVESTMENT THESIS SUMMARY
ANALYST STANCE: BULLISH  |  Palantir is the operating system for AI-native government and enterprise. AIP bootcamp conversion rates of ~80%+ have no historical parallel in enterprise software — every customer that runs a bootcamp is a near-certain contract.
Revenue grew 56% in FY2025, accelerating from 29% in FY2024. US Commercial grew 68%. The AIP flywheel is turning faster than consensus models assume — compounding revenue visibility 3 years out.
Maven Smart System officially designated a Pentagon Program of Record on March 23, 2026 — securing long-term DOD revenue and validating PLTR as the AI backbone of US national defense. This is the bull case materializing.
Base case DCF: $13.65/share at 17.0% WACC. Current price: $154.78 — a 1,034% premium. Bull case (15.5% WACC, 38% CAGR): $22.46. Bear case (18.5% WACC, 18% CAGR): $7.54.
The valuation is extreme by traditional metrics — but PLTR is not a traditional business. Position sizing and entry discipline matter more here than almost any other AI name in the market.

KEY MODEL ASSUMPTIONS & RISKS

1. WACC reflects high beta (2.35x) consistent with high-growth, SBC-heavy tech. Lower beta assumption materially increases target.
2. Terminal growth rate of 4.0% assumes PLTR maintains AI platform leadership and government contract base beyond projection period.
3. SBC headwinds: Adjusted FCF significantly exceeds GAAP FCF due to ~18% SBC/revenue. Model uses GAAP-adjusted FCF.
4. Government concentration risk: ~40% of revenue from US/Int'l government. DOGE-related cuts represent key downside risk.
5. Bull case requires sustained 38%+ CAGR through 2029, implying ~$14B revenue — aggressive but defensible given AI tailwinds.
MARKET IMPLIED ASSUMPTIONS  |  At PLTR's current price of $154.78 (March 2026), the market is implying a WACC of ~8-9% and terminal growth of ~5.5%+ — requiring sustained 38-40% revenue CAGR through 2029 with FCF margins expanding to 42%+. This is achievable if AIP bootcamp adoption continues at current velocity and government AI spending accelerates beyond guidance. My base case assumes deceleration (28% CAGR) — hence the 1,034% gap to current price. The bull case closes this gap. Maven Program of Record designation (3/23/26) is the first major catalyst validating the bull scenario.
